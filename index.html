Karla Olinda

Valentina Karla Olinda Winanta

Karla Olinda is a Balinese-Indonesian singer and theatre performer bringing classical voice, theatrical presence, and hopeful storytelling together. Her work explores the human journey, ritual, identity and transformation. Through her voice, Karla turns emotion into presence and hope into sound.
